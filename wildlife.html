Kenya is a country in East Africa with coastline on the Indian Ocean. It encompasses savannah, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley and mountain highlands. It's also home to wildlife like lions, elephants and rhinos. From Nairobi, the capital, safaris visit the Maasai Mara Reserve, known for its annual wildebeest migrations, and Amboseli National Park, offering views of Tanzania's Mt. Kilimanjaro.

The currency of Kenya is the Kenyan shilling. Kenya has a number of national parks and game reserves that are popular tourist destinations. Maasai Mara National Reserve is located in southwestern Kenya and is known for the annual wildebeest migration. Visitors can see lions, elephants, cheetahs, and other wildlife in their natural habitat.
